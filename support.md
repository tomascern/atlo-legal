# Atlo Support

  ## Frequently Asked Questions

  **What is Atlo?**
  Atlo is an offline world atlas featuring detailed vector and
  satellite maps, country profiles, airport information, and GPS
  positioning — all without an internet connection.

  **Does the app require an internet connection?**
  No. All map data is bundled within the app and works fully offline.

  **How do I restore my purchase?**
  Open the app → Settings → Manage Subscription → Restore Purchases.

  **What is included in Premium?**
  - Full map zoom (up to street level)
  - Dark & Light map theme
  - Label size customization
  - Detailed country profiles
  - GPS data overlay (speed & altitude)
  - Alternate app icon

  **My subscription isn't showing as active. What do I do?**
  Try tapping "Restore Purchases" in the subscription screen. Make
  sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase.

  **How do I cancel my subscription?**
  Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to:
  Settings (iOS) → your name → Subscriptions → Atlo.

  ## Contact
  For any issues not covered above, please reach out by opening an
  issue on our [GitHub page](https://github.com/tomascern/atlo-legal/issues).
